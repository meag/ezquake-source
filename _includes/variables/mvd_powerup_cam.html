mvd_powerup_cam
Description:
Will enable powerupcams, cams will be enabled on every viewport 5 seconds before the powerup spawns.
Type:
boolean
Remarks:
For setting up the viewports cams look at
mvd_pc_view_1-4
and for setting the locations of the cams look at
mvd_pc_quad_1-3
mvd_pc_pent_1-3
ValueDescription
falseTurn OFF
trueTurned ON
